About Us

Who We Are?


Facilitating the purchase of bus or flight tickets has historically been a complex and time-consuming process. Whether it involved traveling to a bus terminal, consulting a travel agent for advance reservations, or enduring long queues, the entire experience was cumbersome. Bookkaru has revolutionized this by introducing e-ticketing technology, simplifying and expediting the process. With our user-friendly application, customers can effortlessly explore various options and swiftly book their tickets. As Pakistan's leading travel and ticketing portal, Bookkaru offers a comprehensive platform allowing users to not only secure bus and airline tickets but also make reservations for hotels, train journeys, event attendance, and explore tours and attractions.
